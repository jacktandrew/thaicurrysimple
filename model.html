Kaiteki Ramen is Jeff Chanchaleune and John Vu. We are an Oklahoma City based food truck.

 

With culinary experience from some of the best Japanese restaurants in Oklahoma City, Chicago, Portland and Houston, we've been able to bring OKC it's very first ramen food truck. One that prides itself on authenticity, uniqueness and quality.

 

We love to travel the world and experience amazing food. What we've done is create dishes inspired by our journey so that you may enjoy them too.

 

Find us around town for some grub!

 

Happy slurping!
